WHAT IS FREE SOFTWARE?

Before we get to know about Richard Stallman we need to know about Free software. Software is the application that is used as a program and other operating information in a computer.

Software works on rules, functions, and steps. A free Software is that which allows the users to study, use, distribute and modify the software.

GNU PROJECT

GNU is a Unix- like operating system which is a free software. It consists of GNU packages (Applications, libraries, developer tools and games). GNU allocates machine resources and talks to the hardware. GNU stands for GNU’S Not Unix.

WHO IS RICHARD STALLMAN?

Richard Stallman is an American Activist and Programmer. He was born on March 16 ,1953. He got interested in computers in a very young age. He used to read manuals of IBM 7094.

He finished his graduation from Harvard University. During that age he worked with, IBM New York Scientific Center, APL, MIT Artificial Intelligence Laboratory.

RICHARD STALLMAN AND HIS INITIATIVES

In the late 1970s, he noticed how companies prevented their software from being used on other competitor’s devices. Many companies started using copyrights and restrictive software licenses to prohibit copying of their source code.

Stallman argued that software users should have the freedom to modify and get the software coding and sources. He campaigned for Free Software. He opened a Free Software Foundation in 1985 to promote freedom of having a right to modify the software. He also made a GNU Project possible in 1983.
